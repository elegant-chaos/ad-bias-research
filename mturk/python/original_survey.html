In this task, you will view screen shots of webpages that include an advertisement placed by a third party service. Each advertisement has a blue box surrounding it. For each webpage/advertisement combination, you will rate how relevant the advertisement is to the webpage based on your subjective judgement. The Oxford English Dictionary defines "relevant" as "closely connected or appropriate to what is being done or considered." So, for each image, you will decide if the advertisement is closely connected or appropriate to the content of the webpage. There are a total of 16 images to rate.

The task ends with a question asking for your opinion on advertisements in general, followed by several demographic questions.
